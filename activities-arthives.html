What is an Art Hive?



An Art Hive, whether located in your neighbourhood or online community, is an inclusive space that welcomes everyone as an artist.





ELLDER Art Hive facilitators celebrate the strengths and creative capacities of seniors through self-directed artmaking, learning and skill-sharing in an intergenerational, imaginative, and safe space. It provides free access and encourages creative reuse of materials.

Through an online platform, the art hive aims to provide each participant with the same sense of creative, inclusive community as they would experience in a physical art hive while staying safe at home.

Please note that you do not need to have previous experience in art to enjoy these activities.
